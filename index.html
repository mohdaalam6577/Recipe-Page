Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teapoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temprrerature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cup semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips

Instruction

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degree
  2. In a small bowl,whisk together the flour and baking
    soda, set aside
  3. In a large bowl, mix the butter, granulated sugar, brown
    sugar, and salt until creamy.
  4. Beat in the agges and varilla until well conbined.
  5. Add the flour mixture and stire in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dought onto bakink sheets.
  7. Bake until the edges are golden but the centers are still
    soft, about 10 minutes
  8. Cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes
    before transfering to wire rackes

Classic Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cup fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 6 8 fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instruction

  1. To make these scrumptious cheesy pizza, divide the pizza dough into 2 portions and roll them into a medium sized rounds on a floured surface.
  2. Place them on a baking sheet separated by a sheet of parchment paper. Freeze until dough is firm.
  3. Heat 1/2 tsp of oil over medium flame. Add the garlic and stir for 30 seconds. Keep aside.
  4. Brush the brinjals with oil and grill till tender and golden brown.Remove and keep aside.
  5. Brush the dough with some oil and garlic. Sprinkle salt and pepper.
  6. Add the brinjals, spinach, cottage cheese, tomato slices and grill till cheese starts to melt.
  7. Drizzle some vinegar and sprinkle basil on top. Serve with your favourite milkshake.
